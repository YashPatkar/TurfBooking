FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common queries from our users.

How do I book a turf?

Simply sign up on our platform, browse available turfs in your area, select your preferred date and time slot, make the payment, and you'll receive instant confirmation. It's that easy!

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes! You can cancel or modify your booking up to 6 hours before your scheduled time. Cancellations made before this time are eligible for a full refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay. All payments are 100% secure.

Are there any hidden charges or booking fees?

Absolutely not! We believe in transparent pricing. The price you see is the price you pay - no hidden charges, no booking fees. What you see is what you get!

What if the turf is not as described?

All turfs on our platform are verified. However, if you face any issues, contact our support team immediately and we'll resolve it. We also have a review system to maintain quality standards.

How do I contact customer support?

Our support team is available 24/7 via email, phone, and live chat. You can reach us through the contact form below or directly from your user dashboard.
